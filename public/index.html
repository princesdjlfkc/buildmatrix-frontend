Pro Tips

Builder's Cheat Sheet

Things every Filipino PC builder should know before spending a single peso.

1
CPU & GPU Balance Matters Most
Pairing a high-end GPU with a weak CPU creates a bottleneck — your FPS suffers. Our builder automatically detects and warns you about imbalanced builds.
2
Buy 80+ Bronze PSU Minimum
Never cheap out on a PSU. A low-quality unit can fry your entire build. Always aim for 80+ Bronze certified or higher, and add 20% headroom over your estimated wattage.
3
16GB RAM is the Sweet Spot
For gaming in 2025, 16GB DDR4 is ideal. 8GB will bottleneck modern titles. 32GB is worth it for content creators and streamers running OBS simultaneously.
4
Check Socket Compatibility
Your CPU and motherboard MUST share the same socket. AMD Ryzen 5000 uses AM4, Ryzen 7000 uses AM5. Intel 12th/13th gen uses LGA1700. Our builder auto-checks this.
5
NVMe SSD Over SATA for Your OS
Put Windows on an NVMe SSD (M.2 slot) for dramatically faster boot times and game load speeds. SATA SSDs are still great for secondary storage.
6
Case Airflow Beats RGB
A case with good airflow keeps temperatures lower and extends component lifespan. Prioritize cases with mesh fronts and multiple fan mounting spots over pure aesthetics.
